Palak Paneer

30 mins
Serves 4
Medium

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh spinach leaves
  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

1

Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2 minutes, then transfer to ice water.

2

Blend spinach with ginger, garlic, and green chilies to make a smooth puree.

3

Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

4

Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.

5

Add spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes.

6

Add paneer cubes and garam masala, mix gently.

7

Add cream and simmer for 3-4 minutes.

8

Garnish with cream and serve hot with roti or rice.
